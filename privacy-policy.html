NeuroFinance Hub ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit neurofinancehub.com, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

01 Information we collect

We collect information in three ways:

  • Information you provide directly — such as your name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter or submit the contact form.
  • Automatically collected data — including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the site, gathered through analytics tools.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — small files stored on your device to remember preferences and measure site usage. See Section 3 for details.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive financial account information, passwords, or government ID numbers through this site.

02 How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send newsletters or content updates you've opted into
  • Respond to messages submitted through our contact form
  • Understand how visitors use the site, so we can improve content and design
  • Detect and prevent fraud, spam, or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable

03 Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. These tools may collect data like your approximate location, device type, and browsing behavior on this site.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so may affect some site functionality, such as remembering newsletter preferences.

04 Third-party services

We may use trusted third-party services to operate this site, including:

  • Email/newsletter providers (e.g., Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or Beehiiv) to manage subscriptions
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) to measure site traffic
  • Hosting providers (GitHub Pages) to serve this website
  • Advertising partners, if and when we run ads or affiliate links — these may use their own cookies subject to their own privacy policies

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

05 Do we sell or share your data?

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data with service providers listed above solely to operate the site (e.g., sending newsletters), and only to the extent necessary for them to perform that function.

06 Your rights & choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link
  • Object to or restrict certain data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 9.

07 Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

08 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

09 Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, reach out at contact@neurofinancehub.com or via our contact page.
